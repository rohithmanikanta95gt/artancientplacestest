Top Historical Places in India

The Taj Mahal

The Taj Mahal, located in Agra, is a UNESCO World Heritage Site and one of the Seven Wonders of the World.

It was built by Mughal Emperor Shah Jahan in memory of his wife Mumtaz Mahal.

Location: Agra, Uttar Pradesh

Timings: 6:00 AM – 6:30 PM (Closed on Fridays)
